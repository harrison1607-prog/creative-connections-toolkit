Guidance

Structured, reusable guidance for student-led networking projects in the creative industries. Organised into five modules that can be expanded across cohorts and institutions.

Use this section to plan and deliver inclusive, university-supported networking initiatives. Each module offers practical steps, checklists, and examples. Content is designed to be non-personal, non-commercial, and suitable for long-term reuse.

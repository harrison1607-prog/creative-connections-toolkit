Reflections

An anonymised learning archive to improve future cohorts’ practice. Contributions should be consent-based and moderated.

This section captures what students found useful, challenging, or missing — written as reusable insight rather than personal opinion. Publish only anonymised contributions with explicit consent.

How to contribute (suggested process)

  • Collect reflections via a consent-based form (e.g., Microsoft/Google Forms).
  • Moderate and anonymise all submissions before publishing.
  • Group insights by theme (e.g., access barriers, confidence, collaboration patterns).
  • Allow contributors to request removal where feasible.
Safeguarding note: do not publish sensitive or identifying information. Where content relates to wellbeing, safety, or discrimination, use appropriate institutional reporting routes and support resources.

Creative Connections Toolkit

A university-supported, reusable toolkit for student-led networking projects in the creative industries, developed by Creative Connections (UCL) in collaboration with The New Black Film Collective.

Purpose

This toolkit provides structured guidance, documented case studies, and reusable resources to support inclusive, student-led networking initiatives across the creative industries.

What This Toolkit Includes

  • Guidance on designing and delivering student-led events
  • Case studies documenting past projects and learning outcomes
  • Templates and checklists for organisers
  • Anonymised reflections and contributions from participants

Institutional Context

This website is maintained as a shared educational resource. It is not a personal portfolio or commercial platform, and is designed for long-term reuse by future student cohorts and partner institutions.
